# 0x243 - Comparison Operators

- Less than
  - `<`
  - `(a < b)`
- Greater than
  - `>`
  - `(a > b)`
- Less than or equal to
  - `<=`
  - `(a <= b)`
- Greater than or equal to
  - `>=`
  - `(a >= b)`
- Equal to
  - `==`
  - `(a == b)`
- Not equal to
  - `!=`
  - `(a != b)`

- Inverting a comparison operator
  - `!(a < b)` is equivalent to `(a >= b)`

- OR
  - `||`
  - `((a < b) || (a < c))`
- AND
  - `&&`
  - `((a < b) && !(a < c))`
